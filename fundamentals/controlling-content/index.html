Controlling Content

ActiveMember360 can control access to both the entire page/post and to specific sections of content within the page/post.

These features alone, or in combination, enable the generation of dynamic, personalized and segmented content for your site.

For more information on that specific topic please visit Dynamic, Personalized & Segmented Content.

Controlling Access To The Entire Page/Post#

Access to the entire page/post is controlled using the settings within the ActiveMember360 Protection panel available when creating or editing the page/post. These settings are very comprehensive and permit virtually any permission condition to be defined.

For a full explanation of the ActiveMember360 Protection panel settings and the behaviour the visitor experiences when they do not have the necessary permissions to view the page/post please visit Controlling Access To & Visibility Of Pages & Posts.

Special Pages#

ActiveMember360 treats certain pages within your WordPress site as special pages.

These pages will be accessible to any visitor to the site.

For more information please visit Special Pages.

Excerpts#

Excerpts are used in certain circumstances when the vistor to a protected page/post does not have the necessary permissions to view the entire page/post.

To gain a fuller understanding of excerpts and how they are used with ActiveMember360 please visit Excerpts.

Custom Post Types#

ActiveMember360, apart from controlling access to standard WordPress post/pages, can also protect most WordPress custom post types.

Generally for ActiveMember360 Extensions (e.g. WooCommerce, LearnDash, bbPress etc.) the custom post type will be automatically supported and the ActiveMember360 Protection panel will be displayed.

However for other custom post types you will need to select in ActiveMember360, Extensions, Custom Post Types the custom post types for which ActiveMember360 will provide protection and the Protection panel will be displayed.

To enable protection for custom post types please visit ActiveMember360, Extensions, Custom Post Types.

Controlling Access To Content Within The Page/Post#

Access to sections of content within a page/post is controlled by conditional ActiveMember360 Shortcodes and also by the ActiveMember360 Extensions for the various page builders, e.g. ActiveMember360 Content Access Settings
